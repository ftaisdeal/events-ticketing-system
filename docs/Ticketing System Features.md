# **Ticketing System Features**

## **User Features**

1. SSL logo
2. Stripe gateway, prominently featured
3. OAuth account creation
4. Mobile optimized
5. PDF of ticket emailed
6. Refund within 7 days of event
7. Login credentials recovery

## **Admin Features**

1. Cookie to store source of initial contact
2. Dashboard to display current ticket sales
3. Transfer funds to B of A
4. Refunds
5. Communication system with customers
6. Export of email addresses
7. Calculate conversation rates
8. Display of rate of ticket sales over time
9. No "cart stuffing" - limit number of tickets per customer
10. Strong concurrency handling to prevent cart stuffing
11. Captcha or other bot blocker
12. Velocity checker (fast actions are not by humans)
13. Fraud heuristics
14. IP blocker + lists of bad IPs
